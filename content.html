Content

In this course, we explored the comprehensive process of content creation, covering pre-production, production, and post-production stages. We gained hands-on experience in planning, shooting, and editing content while learning the importance of each phase in delivering high-quality results. During the pre-production stage, we focused on preparation, including planning shots, setting up equipment, and organizing interviews.

In the production phase, we experimented with filming interviews, capturing high-quality video and audio while learning techniques to enhance visual storytelling. The post-production stage involved editing video, enhancing images, and refining audio using industry-standard tools such as Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Audition, and After Effects. We also experimented with color grading and visual effects, gaining insight into how these elements contribute to polished and engaging content.

Redesign of a website

For this assignment, we redesigned a company’s website by interviewing the owner of a local antique store and capturing photos and videos. Using research methods, prototypes, testing, wireframes, sitemaps, and layout diagrams, we streamlined the process to ensure the design met both the business’s goals and the needs of its users.
