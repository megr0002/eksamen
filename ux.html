UX - User experience

In this course, we explored a variety of research methods to uncover user needs, behaviors, and motivations. These techniques provided valuable insights into understanding the customer or user base, as well as the businesses and their competitors. By analyzing this information, we gained a deeper comprehension of the factors influencing user experiences and decision-making processes.

This knowledge is crucial for creating user-friendly designs that effectively address the needs of both users and businesses. It enables designers to strike a balance between user satisfaction and business objectives, resulting in solutions that are not only functional and appealing but also strategically aligned with the goals of the organization.
