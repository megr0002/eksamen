About me

Hi there! I'm Melanie Grath, a 21-year-old multimedia design student based in Slagelse, Denmark. I’ve always had a deep passion for creativity, which fuels both my work and my personal life. Whether I’m sketching in my notebook, experimenting with graphic design, or diving into a new book or movie, I’m constantly inspired by the world around me.

Currently, I’m studying multimedia design, where I get to blend my love for design with my technical skills. Graphic design, in particular, holds a special place in my heart, and I’m always eager to push my creative boundaries and explore new ways to express visual ideas.

When I’m not immersed in design projects, you’ll find me enjoying a good book, taking care of my plants, cooking, drawing or getting lost in a captivating movie. These hobbies help me unwind and spark fresh creative ideas that I bring into my work. Design isn't just what I do—it’s who I am. I’m always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow, and I’d love to collaborate on exciting projects that bring ideas to life.
